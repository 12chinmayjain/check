About Me

As I grew, I transitioned into a full-stack role — now I handle everything from backend development to frontend integration and testing.

But through persistence and continuous learning, I adapted, delivered, and grew tremendously — even earning a recognition award from my team.

My programming roots go back to college, where I started with C++ and then shifted to Python for its simplicity and versatility. I explored everything from DSA to building projects like Cars (a car filtering system based on user needs) and Weblocks (a site builder using given templates).

Currently, I’m strengthening my core in web development and revisiting data structures & algorithms to stay sharp. I find genuine satisfaction in working with new technologies and delivering projects that meet client expectations with precision.
